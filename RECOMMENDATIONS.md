## ✅ High Impact / Easy to Add

### 1. Social Media Links (Facebook + Instagram)
- **Impact**: High - Every upscale sushi site has these
- **Effort**: Low - Inline SVGs, no new dependencies
- **Placement**: Footer brand column + Location/Contact section

### 2. Desserts Section
- **Impact**: Medium - Rounds out the menu
- **Effort**: Low - Photos already exist (mochi, tiramisu)
- **Placement**: New menu category after Beverages

### 3. Email Newsletter Signup
- **Impact**: Medium - Builds mailing list for promotions
- **Effort**: Low - Simple form field in footer
- **Example**: "Join our guest list for exclusive invitations and seasonal announcements"

---

## 🎨 Medium Impact / Moderate Effort

### 4. Drinks / Sake Menu
- **Impact**: High - Signals "sit-down experience" vs. "takeout spot"
- **Effort**: Medium - Requires menu data, photos optional
- **Content**: Sake, Japanese whisky, cocktails, wine list
- **Example**: "One of Utah's largest Japanese whisky and sake programs"

### 5. Chef / Team Profile
- **Impact**: High - Builds trust and personal connection
- **Effort**: Medium - Requires chef bio, photo, background
- **Placement**: New "About the Chef" section
- **Content**: Training, philosophy, experience

### 6. Private Dining / Events Section
- **Impact**: High - Revenue driver
- **Effort**: Medium - Requires event inquiry form or email
- **Content**: Capacity info, space photos, contact form

### 7. Press / Awards / Accolades
- **Impact**: Medium - Social proof
- **Effort**: Medium - Requires gathering mentions, quotes
- **Content**: "Best of Utah," Yelp awards, local press, food blog features

### 8. Gift Cards
- **Impact**: Medium - Revenue driver
- **Effort**: Low - Link to Square/Toast portal or "Available in-store"
- **Placement**: Nav bar (prominent at Nobu, Uchi, O Ya)

### 9. Instagram Feed Embed
- **Impact**: Medium - Shows restaurant is active, food looks amazing
- **Effort**: Low - Simple embed code
- **Placement**: Below gallery or above footer

### 10. Lunch Specials Section
- **Impact**: Medium - Drives weekday traffic
- **Effort**: Medium - Requires menu data
- **Placement**: New section or prominent callout

---

## 🚀 Aspirational / Lower Priority

### 11. Omakase / Chef's Tasting Menu
- **Impact**: Very High - #1 signal of "upscale"
- **Effort**: High - Requires offering the service
- **Content**: Course count, price, reservation info
- **Note**: Centerpiece of sites like NAOE, Sushi Nakazawa, Masa

### 12. FAQ Page
- **Impact**: Low - Reduces phone calls
- **Effort**: Medium
- **Content**: Hours, parking, dress code, allergy policies, large party guidelines

### 13. Accessibility Statement
- **Impact**: Low - Good practice, increasingly expected
- **Effort**: Low
- **Placement**: Footer link

### 14. Catering Menu
- **Impact**: Medium - New revenue stream
- **Effort**: Medium
- **Content**: Sushi platters for events/offices

### 15. Virtual Tour / More Ambiance Photos
- **Impact**: Medium - Helps customers picture themselves there
- **Effort**: High - Requires professional photography
- **Note**: Current gallery only shows food - no interior shots

### 16. Careers Page
- **Impact**: Low
- **Effort**: Low
- **Content**: "Join our team" with email address

### 17. Sustainability / Sourcing Story
- **Impact**: Medium - Differentiator if Hikari has a story
- **Effort**: Medium
- **Content**: Where fish comes from, freshness commitments
- **Examples**: NAOE highlights family soy sauce brewery; Sushi Den flies fish from Japan
